Course
  ├── title, description, price, level
  ├── instructor (ForeignKey to User)
  ├── students (ManyToMany to User)
  └── created_at, updated_at

Module (belongs to Course)
  ├── title, description, order
  └── course (ForeignKey to Course)

Lesson (belongs to Module)
  ├── title, content_type (video/text/quiz)
  ├── content (JSON for different types)
  ├── video_url, duration (if video)
  ├── quiz_data (if quiz)
  ├── order, is_free_preview
